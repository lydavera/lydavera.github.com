Welcome!

Computerized electrodermal screening measures the degree of electrical resistance at specific acupuncture points on the fingers and toes, recording galvanic skin resistance. There are no no needles, electrical shocks, or invasive techniques used. The computer converts the skin resistance response to a number. The range of numbers indicates balance and imbalance. A print out and explanation is included with each client's report following their screening information. This system is marketed as a non-invasive, biofeedback or Tens device, which screens for variations in galvanic skin resistance.
