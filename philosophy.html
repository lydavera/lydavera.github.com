Philosophy

The Age of Environmental Illnesses

For the past several years I have had the good fortune to work with doctors – medical and alternative – and have screened a large number of people who were severely sick and could no longer function efficiently in their daily lives. I have a specialized computer system which enables me to screen the balance and imbalances in body organs, glands and environmental sensitivities including foods, chemicals, dusts, pollens, pets, etc. The system I currently use is called the EQ4/Listen 10 and this system is approved with the Health Protection Bureau in Canada, as a class 2 medical device. I began working on the Interro, then moved onto the Omega Acubase and changed to my present system. I've also worked with the Kindling 2000 devices.

The medical and alternative doctors that I have had the opportunity to work with find this screening very helpful. It gives additional information to their protocols which doesn't always show up with blood, urine and hair analysis. For some types of blood and urine analysis the patient must show specific levels of specific chemicals for a problem to be detected. Bioenergetic screening will show imbalances before a chronic level is evident or will confirm stress or under functioning for specific organs and glands. It doesn't specify amounts of physiological substances in the body, but will confirm if there are sensitivities: high, low, or balanced, by a number scale.

When an individual gets to the point of being unwell and can't get over it, there are a number of ways to look at this problem.

  • Find a medical doctor who you have confidence in and have a complete medical exam and whatever tests are recommended. There can be hidden illnesses which haven't fully materialized and it is important to eliminate what can be eliminated. Listen and follow their protocol, for the time specified.
  • It is unraveling a puzzle, beginning with medical and family history - genetic sensitivity/predisposition's, past or present illnesses/weaknesses.
  • Look at your lifestyle:
    • Nutrition
    • Home Environment
    • Work Environment
    • Relaxation
    • Exercise

Nutrition

Types of food, chemical additives, pesticides, irradiation. An individual can say, "I am eating well and I don't abuse myself with junk food and alcohol or recreational drugs." The underlying problem can be a weakened digestive system, with imbalanced biochemistry to digest properly. This translates to poor nutrition even if eating perfectly. It is important to determine the status of the digestive system, for assimilation and absorption. Vitamin and Mineral supplements often go right through the body without breaking down. This costs a lot of money for nothing and to no avail. Once the body gets stronger and back on track a lot of supplementation can be eliminated. The next step is to determine what foods and nutrients we need to benefit from.

Important Note

It is not easy, because a lot of people do not want to be bothered with lifestyle changes. "Just give me a pill or a quick fix, so I can keep on with the life I like to lead."

Home Environment

Some individuals become so sensitive to their environment that everything bothers them. This includes, pets, carpets, wall paint, construction materials, odors, perfumes, literally everything. Family stress/emotions directly effect specific organs and glands as well. As a result, it becomes difficult to identify or pinpoint any specific item. From the point of screening I usually concentrate on the body systems and glands and foods. Once a person is chronically sensitized, the immune system is almost non-functioning. There is usually an extreme toxic overload in the body and nutritional deficiency. A myriad of symptoms will result, such as depression, eczema, diarrhea, fatigue, chronic fatigue, migraines, mood swings, anger, etc. The body is in a complete state of stress and from my screening perspective all I can get is stress readings. A beginning step is to have the electrodermal screening done to determine your specific "blueprint" of energetic balances and imbalances.

Important Note

Each individual is unique and may need specific treatments. It is important to follow the program of a professional doctor or naturopath/homeopath for guidance when in a state of extreme illness.

Work Environment

Our work place is another myriad of influences that can benefit or inhibit our sense of well being. What are the influences from your work place? Chemicals, fluorescent lighting, computer's electromagnetic fields, high tension wires, perfumes, animals, emotional stress, hospital sicknesses, foods/chemical additives, dust, stress, stress, stress. Stress alone translates into many different emotions and again specific organs and glands receive negative information and the functioning of these systems become impaired.

Relaxation

Learning how to relax these days without alcohol and medical or recreational drugs is almost a lost art. There are many schools on this subject, of course. One of the most effective ways to relax is learning how to breathe properly and visualization techniques. Each person will find their own way to relax depending on our personalities and requirements.

If you are a person who travels out of the country, it is possible to pick up amoebaes, parasites, and other organisms. Confirmation and treatment is essential for wellbeing.

Exercise

The awareness of getting in shape physically is obvious and a personal choice. It is unfortunate in this age of computers and videos that we are not outside enough and not breathing deeply enough. Some doctors I have worked with believe that the sun and country side with flowers and trees feed a level of our being that stimulates our master glands which in turn balance our body systems.

Summation

So far we have looked at the medical/genetic aspect, nutrition, home environment and the work environment, relaxation, and exercise. Combinations of these may contribute to "environmental illness."

Generally speaking we live too much inside artificial environments and eat artificial foods, all with good intent and a lot of the time not realizing how all this is effecting us until we get ill to the point of almost no return. Usually by one month changes are apparent and wellness is starting to become stronger and stronger. It is not easy trusting this advice because it is not a quick fix.
